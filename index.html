List

Manage your #to-do list in a simple way with @Piece.

Remember: it’s just for temporary use

Piece keeps your temporary content in a #single view, rather than a txt or doc.

And it’s extremely easy to use

Press #Shift+Alt+S to show/hide Piece @anywhere and @anytime.

Less = More

The product philosophy of Piece is #LESS_equals_MORE.

It doesn’t matter where we put ours thoughts, ideas and to-dos down. What really counts is that we actually #do that eventually - go back to my thoughts and work out some problems; continue my new idea and make it big; or just simply get everything listed done. The way how we put the pieces down should be as simple as possible. And this simple way, with your pieces is the #LESS, which might bring your something #MORE than you have imagined.

And this is what #Piece do.
